Help

RobustQ is a web service to quantify cellular robustness of metabolic models. This is done by enumerating the number of minimal cut-sets (MCS) and then calculating the probability of failure (PoF) of a given network. You can learn more about the method here.

How to get started:

  1. Create an account or Sign In
  2. Queue up your job by uploading a valid SBML model and setting the desired parameters.
  3. Wait for the job to finish. This may take up to 24 hours, depending on the model size.
  4. It may happen that your job fails. Please check the logs for further details or try tweaking parameters.
